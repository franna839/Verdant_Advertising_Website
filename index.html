The Hardware

The Verdant Node

A low-cost, sensor hub that provides real-time monitoring of crucial environmental conditions inside your greenhouse or tunnel.

  • Real-Time Data Collection

    Collects crucial environmental data like temperature, humidity, and CO2 levels.

  • Adaptive Feedback Response

    Activates resources like a fan automatically when extreme conditions such as high temperature are detected.

  • User-Friendly Alerts

    Sends actionable alerts to your device when conditions go outside your custom thresholds.
